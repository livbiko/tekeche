Service Level Agreement

This Service Level Agreement (SLA) defines the service levels, response times, and support commitments that LivBiko provides to its managed service clients.

Last updated: 1 January 2023

1. Service Availability

LivBiko commits to a 99.9% uptime target for managed infrastructure services (excluding scheduled maintenance windows), measured on a monthly basis.

Scheduled maintenance will be conducted outside of core business hours (Mon–Fri, 08:00–18:00 GMT) wherever possible, with a minimum of 48 hours' written notice to the Client.

2. Incident Priority Levels

All incidents raised with LivBiko are classified into one of four priority levels:

  • Priority 1 – Critical: Complete service outage or security breach affecting the entire business. Response: 15 minutes. Resolution target: 4 hours.
  • Priority 2 – High: Major functionality impaired, significant number of users affected, or security incident contained but active. Response: 1 hour. Resolution target: 8 hours.
  • Priority 3 – Medium: Partial service degradation, single user or non-critical system affected. Response: 4 hours. Resolution target: 2 business days.
  • Priority 4 – Low: General enquiries, minor requests, and changes with no immediate business impact. Response: 1 business day. Resolution target: 5 business days.

3. Support Hours

Standard managed service clients receive support during core business hours: Monday to Friday, 08:00–18:00 GMT, excluding UK public holidays.

Clients on our 24/7 Enhanced plan receive support at all hours, including weekends and public holidays, for Priority 1 and Priority 2 incidents.

Out-of-hours support for lower-priority incidents can be arranged at an additional cost, as defined in your Service Agreement.

4. Response Channels

LivBiko accepts incidents and service requests via the following channels:

  • Telephone: +442035189077 (Priority 1 and 2 — always call for critical issues)
  • Email: info@livbiko.com
  • Client Portal: tickets raised via the portal are reviewed within business hours

5. Exclusions

The following are excluded from SLA commitments:

  • Incidents caused by third-party services outside LivBiko's control (e.g. internet provider outages, Microsoft or Oracle platform incidents).
  • Incidents resulting from changes made by the Client without LivBiko's involvement or approval.
  • Incidents caused by force majeure events including natural disasters, pandemic conditions, or civil unrest.
  • Scheduled maintenance windows agreed in advance with the Client.

6. Service Credits

If LivBiko fails to meet the response or resolution targets defined above due to causes within our reasonable control, the Client may be entitled to service credits as defined in the Service Agreement.

Service credits are the Client's sole financial remedy for SLA failures and do not affect the Client's right to terminate for material breach where applicable.

Service credits are applied as a deduction against future invoices and are capped at 10% of the monthly service fee for the affected month.

7. Reporting

LivBiko provides monthly service reports covering: total tickets raised, resolution within SLA, average response and resolution times, system availability, and any notable incidents.

Reports are provided via the client portal and reviewed at quarterly business review meetings.
