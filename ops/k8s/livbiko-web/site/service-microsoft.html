The Service

One partner. Your entire Microsoft stack.

Microsoft 365 and Azure are the backbone of most modern businesses — but managing them properly requires expertise across licensing, security, identity, device management, and infrastructure. Most IT teams don't have time to do it all well.

As a Microsoft Partner, LivBiko manages your entire Microsoft environment directly. We work inside your tenants — not at arm's length — handling everything from day-to-day administration to complex migrations and security hardening.

Tenant Management & Administration

We manage your Microsoft 365 and Azure tenants directly — user lifecycle, group policies, licensing optimisation, and security defaults — keeping everything clean, compliant, and cost-efficient.

Azure Infrastructure

Design, deployment, and ongoing management of Azure resources: virtual machines, storage, virtual networking, Azure SQL, App Services, and more — with cost governance built in from day one.

Microsoft 365 Migration

Full migrations from on-premises Exchange, Google Workspace, or legacy platforms to Microsoft 365 — email, calendars, contacts, OneDrive, and SharePoint — with zero data loss.

Entra ID & Conditional Access

Identity management, MFA enforcement, conditional access policies, and Privileged Identity Management (PIM) — ensuring only the right people access the right resources.

Intune Device Management

Enrolment, policy configuration, and compliance monitoring for Windows, iOS, Android, and macOS devices — enforcing security baselines and enabling remote wipe when needed.

Teams & SharePoint Governance

Structure your Microsoft Teams tenancy, design SharePoint architecture, manage permissions, and implement lifecycle policies — so your collaboration environment stays clean and secure.
