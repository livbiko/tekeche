Hosted Virtual Desktop

Secure, fully managed cloud-hosted virtual desktops delivered via Cisco AnyConnect VPN and Remote Desktop Protocol — giving your team a consistent, enterprise-grade workspace from any device, anywhere in the world.

Cisco AnyConnectRemote DesktopVDIZero-Trust AccessCloud-HostedAlways-On
The Service

Your desktop, in the cloud. Your team, anywhere.

Traditional desktops tie your workforce to a physical location and burden your IT team with hardware maintenance, patching, and end-of-life replacements. LivBiko's Hosted Virtual Desktop eliminates all of that.

We build and manage your entire desktop environment in the cloud, delivered securely over Cisco AnyConnect VPN. Users connect via Remote Desktop from any device — laptop, tablet, or thin client — and get the same high-performance, fully patched workspace every time.

Cisco AnyConnect VPN Connectivity

Every virtual desktop session is established over Cisco AnyConnect, enforcing multi-factor authentication, device compliance checks, and encrypted tunnelling before any data is accessible.

Remote Desktop Protocol (RDP) Management

Full management of Windows virtual desktop environments delivered via RDP — provisioning, patching, performance tuning, and user lifecycle all handled by our team.

User Onboarding in Minutes

Adding a new user takes minutes, not days. We provision accounts, apply group policies, install applications, and have a new starter up and running before their first morning.

Application Management

We package and deploy your business applications — Office 365, line-of-business software, and bespoke tools — centrally, so every user always has the correct, up-to-date version.

Session Security & Audit Logging

All sessions are encrypted in transit. Data never leaves the hosted environment. Full audit logs and optional session recording are available for regulated industries.

24/7 Monitoring & Support

We monitor your hosted desktop environment around the clock — performance, availability, and security — with helpdesk support available whenever your team needs it.

Why It Matters

Why Hosted Virtual Desktop?

01

No More Hardware Refresh Cycles

Eliminate the cost and disruption of PC replacements. Your users work from any device — new or old — while we maintain the environment in the cloud.

02

Work From Anywhere, Securely

Cisco AnyConnect ensures your team can connect securely from home, on the road, or at a client site — without compromising on security or performance.

03

Predictable Monthly Cost

No capital expenditure on hardware, no surprise maintenance bills. One flat monthly fee covers your entire hosted desktop environment.

04

Instant Disaster Recovery

Because your desktops live in the cloud, losing a physical device is a minor inconvenience — not a crisis. Users are back up and running on a new device in minutes.
