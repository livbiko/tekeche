Our Story

We are LivBiko

Founded by engineers who believed that businesses should never have to worry about their IT — built on deep technical expertise, genuine partnership, and a relentless focus on keeping your systems running.

Our Mission

Built to bridge the gap between ambition and infrastructure

Too many businesses deal with reactive IT, surprise bills, and vendors who disappear after the sale. We built LivBiko to be the opposite — a fully managed partner who monitors, maintains, and improves your IT environment proactively, every single day.

We manage Microsoft Azure, Office 365, Oracle OCI, hosted virtual desktops, cybersecurity, and disaster recovery — all under one roof, with one team you can actually call.

Our Vision

A world where every business — regardless of size — has access to enterprise-grade managed IT, cloud infrastructure, and security, without needing an in-house IT department to run it.

Our Commitment

99.9% uptime SLAs, 24/7 helpdesk, sub-1-hour response times, and a dedicated account team certified across Microsoft, Oracle, and Cisco platforms.

Our Culture

Proactive by default. We do not wait for things to break — we monitor, patch, and improve your environment continuously so your team never has to raise a ticket for the same issue twice.
